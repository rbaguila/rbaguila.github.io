About Me

I am a fullstack web developer knowledgeable in PHP and JavaScript frameworks. I have experienced developing web applications using HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node, Express, MeteorJS, Ruby on Rails, Python, MySQL, MongoDB. I have used NGINX, Apache and Tomcat server for deployment and maintenance. I have managed and supervised a different development team in my years of experience.

I also have training and teaching experience in the following courses: "Introduction to the Internet", "Introduction to Computer Science", "Basics of Mobile Development (Android Native)" and "Web Programming". I have years of practice in developing applications in the industry and research field since I graduated BS Computer Science April of 2013 and Masters of Information Technology December of 2016.

Currently, I'm exploring the technologies behind web3. I have experience developing smart contract in the Ethreum and Polygon blockchains. I recently spearheaded the website development, art generation, smart contract development, and minting app development of Kusho World.
